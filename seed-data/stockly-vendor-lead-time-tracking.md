**Product:** Stockly
**Category:** AI-powered inventory & demand forecasting for retail and e-commerce

## Summary

Stockly plugs into a retailer's POS, ERP, and e-commerce platforms to forecast
demand at the SKU x location level, then automates replenishment.

## Features

- **Vendor Lead Time Tracking** — Learns each vendor's actual historical lead time
  (not their quoted lead time) and adjusts reorder points automatically, instead of
  using a single static lead-time assumption across all vendors.

## Proof Points

- Customers using Vendor Lead Time Tracking saw **emergency freight costs drop
  33%**, since reorder points now account for vendors who consistently ship later
  than quoted.

## Roadmap Decision Log

- **2026-07-09** — Decided to track lead time per vendor-per-SKU rather than
  per-vendor-overall, after pilot data showed the same vendor's lead time varying
  significantly by product category.
