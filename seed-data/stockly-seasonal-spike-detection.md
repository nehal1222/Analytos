**Product:** Stockly
**Category:** AI-powered inventory & demand forecasting for retail and e-commerce

## Summary

Stockly plugs into a retailer's POS, ERP, and e-commerce platforms to forecast
demand at the SKU x location level, then automates replenishment so stores and
warehouses stop guessing.

## Features

- **Seasonal Spike Detection** — Automatically identifies recurring seasonal
  demand spikes (holidays, back-to-school, regional events) from up to three
  years of historical sell-through, and pre-positions inventory ahead of the
  spike instead of reacting to it after stockouts start.

## Proof Points

- Pilot retailers using Seasonal Spike Detection **cut holiday-season
  stockouts by 41%** compared to the prior year's unassisted forecasting.

## Roadmap Decision Log

- **2026-07-09** — Decided to ship Seasonal Spike Detection as part of the
  core Demand Forecasting Engine rather than a separate paid add-on, since
  every retail customer has some form of seasonality and gating it behind
  an upsell made the base product's forecasts look worse than they actually
  are.
